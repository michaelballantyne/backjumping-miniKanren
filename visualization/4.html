A broken idea for min jump

Will asked why we couldn't just not put a version number on unifications made in a position that appears to be in the direct left hand side of a conjunction in order to achieve the same as min-jup. We can't do that because there may be another conjunction at a higher level that will use that unification as a jump target, even if it shouldn't be a target for elements in the RHS of the nearest conjunction. The unification in version 1 below is a good example.
